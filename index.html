RED PANDA

SCIENTIFIC NAME: Ailurus fulgens

HABITAT: Eastern Himalayas in places like China, Nepal, and Bhutan.

FOOD: Leaves and bamboo, fruit, insects, bird eggs and small lizard.

SIZE: 51-63.5cm(length) and 3.2-15kg(weight)

FUN FACT: They were given the name 'panda' first; roughly fifty years before the black and white variety.
